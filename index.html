    Hi, Internet!

    I am Julia and I'm pleased to meet you! Originally from Russia, where I graduated with a degree in Linguistics and Education, I moved to Finland in 2012 to pursue education in Experience Management. After wrapping up my studies, I spent several years working in the field of experience creation and design in the event and entertainment industry.

    Somewhere along the way I discovered a passion for coding and decided to become a web developer. Development combines a lot of the things I am passionate about: problem solving, creativity, logical thinking, curiosity, teamwork. It's an adventurous and exciting world of endless brain-teasers and digital mysteries. Sometimes crazy, sometimes extremely challenging, often frustrating, but, most importantly, never boring. That's why I love it :)
